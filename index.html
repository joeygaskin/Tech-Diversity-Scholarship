The Tech Diversity Scholarship is the product of a public-private partnership of organizations committed to providing access to technology education for all. The scholarship is designed to remove financial barriers for people who are underrepresented in the technology industry, including but not limited to: women, people of color, military personnel and people with disabilities. Financially-eligible students will be considered and may be eligible to receive a full-ride scholarship to attend a full-time code school and take the first step toward a fulfilling career in technology.
